The Pop Art Movement Era

Learn the History of Pop Art

The Pop Art movement was born in Britain in the 1950s, from a young artist group who called themselves the Independent group.
The Independent group started a new movement around the world, with this new art. Pop Art was the start of something new for graffiti, comic books, cartoons and more.
This new art didn't focus on making art realistic, in fact art became more of an imaginary world.

City Art

This Pop Art movement spread through the city streets, on buildings, street signs, and part of the underground world.
This new art movement not only influenced the art world, but also started a collaboration in the music industry, and films.
